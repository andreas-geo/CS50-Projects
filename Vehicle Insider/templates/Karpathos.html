Karpathos Island



Karpathos is known as the wild paradise of the Dodecanese.The island just seems to be untouched by industrialization and the the change of the way of life . Visitors can find some of the finest beaches in Europe where they can enjoy crystal clear waters and find peace and quiet.Except from beaches there is a very rich natural landscape that travellers can enjoy through organized hiking,or by renting our Jeep Wrangler, so you can see all the beautiful wild beauties that the island has to offer.
