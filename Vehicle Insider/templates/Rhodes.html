Rhodes Island



Rhodes is one of the most popular islands in Greece.You can choose this destination for many reasons.Classic touristic places like the Castle of the Knights of Rhodes,or maybe religious tourism or even for the island's casino and big clubs and venues.With a car you can escape the main city of Rhodes and enjoy some relaxing moments in villages like Lindos with its beautiful Acropolis or maybe a place like the Kallithea springs where some of the most popular Greek movies have been filmed.
